ABOUT US

We are a full-service Barber and Beauty Salon, the only one of it’s kind in the Harbor City. We take great pride in our craft and it shows with all the satisfied clients we have. We joke with each other that we have more than 200 years of experience between all of us, so we are slightly younger than our constitution! It’s funny, but the real benefit is to our clients, because you get the best of the best. We consult and rely on each other, as professionals do. No EGOS here, just good work. Come experience us, you’ll be glad you did.
